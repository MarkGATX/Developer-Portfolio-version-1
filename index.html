About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio. Or at least the beginning of my Portfolio. Portfolio version 1.0, if you will.

While I've done some coding in the past, becoming a full-time developer is a new challenge. Once the Pandemic hit and lockdowns took effect, I became a full-time Dad to my three-year old son. Before that I was working in digital video and streaming as a writer and producer. While most of my work consisted of personal projects, I did pick the occassional corporate gig to shoot and cut product demos.

Since I don't have a huge backlog of development projects to show off, my main link here is to my Github, where you can see the projects I've been working on as a full-time student in the University of Texas Full Stack Development Bootcamp. Since I had the space I thought I might as well go ahead and feature some of the video projects I've produced and been a part of in the past.

Thanks for taking the time to check things out here and here's looking forward to more devlopment projects and Portfolio version 2.0 and beyond.
